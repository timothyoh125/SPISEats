BBQ Seitan Strips

Here are your Ingredients:

  • 1 cup vital wheat gluten (you can buy this at health food shops or online - the internet is a magical place)
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast (yeah, you’ll be able to buy this at the same place at the vital wheat gluten)
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • a good pinch of black pepper
  • 1 tsp liquid smoke (yeah, this too)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3/4 cup vegetable stock
  • 1/2 cup bbq sauce

Here are your Instructions:

  1. Put all the ingredients (apart from the bbq sauce) in a bowl & mix it together into a ball. Don’t be afraid to get your hands in there & knead it if it’s misbehaving
  2. Pop the mixture onto a lightly greased oven tray & stretch it into the desired shape - if it’s a little unruly, don’t be surprised
  3. Pop the dish into the oven & bake for 20 minutes at 180℃ (356℉)
  4. Take the dish out, brush with the bbq sauce & bake for a further 5 minutes
  5. Take out the oven, cut into “ribs” & serve immediately with fries, roast tomatoes & a pot of bbq sauce for dipping
