Ramen

Here are your Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of Top Ramen
  • 1 egg

Here are your Instructions:

  1. Crack one egg into a bowl for every packet of ramen you are cooking.
  2. Boil water in a saucepan.
  3. Pour the egg into the ramen broth slowly, stirring constantly.
  4. Allow the mixture to cook for another 2minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Turn off the heat and stir in the seasoning packet.
