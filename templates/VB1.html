Red Pepper Pasta

Here are your Ingredients:

  • Splash of olive oil
  • 2 red peppers (deseeded & chopped into chunks)
  • 1 red onion (chopped)
  • 2 red chillies (deseeded) - If you like your food super spicy, add more chillies. If you're not keen on heat, don't add chillies!
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • salt
  • pepper

Here are your Instructions:

  1. Oil a baking tray & put the roasting ingredients on it
  2. Bake the roasting ingredients for 30 minutes at 180℃ (356℉)
  3. Take the vegetable out of the oven, let them cool down a bit, put them in the blender, pour in the almond milk & whizz them all up into a liquid
  4. Pour the liquid into a high sided frying pan, add the cornflour & nutritional yeast & stir everything together
  5. Add the cooked pasta into the pan & stir it all around so the pasta is well covered
  6. Serve immediately & garnish with basil leaves
